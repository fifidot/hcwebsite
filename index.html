As a qualified Health Coach with over 30 years experience in teaching and education and a lifelong passion for nutrition and health, I offer individual health coaching, health and wellbeing group workshops and bespoke corporate programmes.

Health coaching may be for you if you want to improve any aspects of your health that are negatively impacting your daily life, you want to understand the root cause, and are ready to create healthy, doable and sustainable daily habits.

Group health and wellbeing workshops are available for groups of up to 10 people. Popular workshops include Decoding Stress, Brain Health, Healthy Ageing, Keeping Well and Hormone Health.

Bespoke corporate programmes can be designed to cover different areas relevant to the organisation and requirements of the participants.

Every individual's journey to optimal health is unique, shaped by personal genetics, lifestyle choices, and overall well-being. Your path to vibrant, active living is not about adding years to life, but adding life to your years.
